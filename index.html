About Me

I spent past 1.8 years designing data modeling web application at R2.ai.Inc in Bay Area. Before that, I finished my joint Masters of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) at UCLIC in London, and KTH in Sweden. I'm a proud EIT alumni. Prior to that I'm a Computer Sciense student with another degree in Art and Design at SYSU in China. As a product designer, I am good at using design research methods to uncover user’s pains and needs, as well as using visual design skills to turn complex problems into simple and elegant designs.

I am open for freelancing project and also looking for my next role in a passionate design team with a great culture, where I could thrive, be mentored and make friends. Hit me up if you've got interesting projects!

When I’m not designing for or researching the users, you can find me either explore museums or do all sorts of sports: (Hiking 🏔, basketball ⛹, Climbing 🧗, Swimming 🏊, Running 👟.)
