BeTinker

BeTinker is a project in which we seek to build an application that helps elder people play with digital gadgets without coding complex programmes. This project explored the user group of retiree and designed an alternative to Arduino to let the users speak out their voice. The outcome was an tablet based application that enables retirees to learn and play with updated technologe without mastering advantaged coding skills. A workshop was held to test the prototypes and let them express their opinions, innovative ideas about this new maker toolkit.

Time: 2014.9 - 2014.11

Type: Teamwork with 4, Coursework

Role: Team leader, interactive prototyper, workshop organizer

Method & Skill: Role play, affinity diagram, concept mapping, low-fi and hi-fi prototype, video prototype, think aloud evaluation.
